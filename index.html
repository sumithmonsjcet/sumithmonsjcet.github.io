    St. Joseph's College of Engineering and Technology, Palai



    Vision and Mission

    Vision

    To emerge as a center of excellence in the field of computer education with a distinct identity and quality in all areas of its activities, and to develop a new generation of computer professionals with proper leadership, commitment, and moral values.

    Mission

    • Provide quality education in Computer Applications and bridge the gap between the academia and industry.
    • Promote innovative research and leadership in areas relevant to the socio-economic progress of the country.
    • Develop intellectual curiosity and a commitment to lifelong learning in students, with societal and environmental concerns.
